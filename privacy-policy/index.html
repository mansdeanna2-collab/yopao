THE STAMPDEALS Privacy Policy

By visiting this website you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice. This Privacy Notice applies to the StampDeals website. It does not apply to other sites operated by StampDeals or to any third-party websites to which we may link. As our business continues to evolve, this Privacy Notice may change, so please check back periodically for updates.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USAGE

(a) Registration and Ordering

In order for you to purchase products on our site, you must first complete a registration form. During registration you are required to give your name, address, phone number, and email address. We use this information to contact you about the services on our site in which you have expressed interest, as well as for billing and shipping purposes. When ordering, you are required to provide a credit card number and billing address. This information is used for billing purposes only and will not be sold or redistributed to third parties.

(b) Email Addresses

We collect email addresses in order to send you newsletters, promotional notices, and other information about StampDeals products and services. You may opt out of receiving these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each email.

(c) Cookies and Other Technology

We use a variety of technologies on our site to collect information that helps us improve your online experience.

(i) Cookies. Cookies are small data files that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services.

(ii) Web beacons. We may also use web beacons (also known as pixel tags or clear GIFs) on our site or in our emails. Web beacons are tiny graphic images used to track the movement of users through our site or to record whether an email is opened and acted upon.

(iii) Third-party advertising. We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to our website in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

(d) Log Files

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movements around the site, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

(e) Age

Our website is directed to adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

(f) Product Reviews

When you submit a product review on our website, the information you provide (including your name and review text) may be publicly displayed. Please be aware that any information you submit in a review may be read, collected, or used by others.

Information Use and Disclosure

(a) Internal Use

We use your information internally to operate, provide, develop, and improve the products and services that we offer our customers. This includes processing your orders, maintaining your account, and providing customer service.

(b) Communications with You

We use your information to communicate with you about your orders, products and services you have purchased or shown interest in, updates to our policies and terms, and other matters. We may also send you promotional messages and offers. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

(c) External Use

(i) We do not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent.

(ii) We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

(iii) We may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

(iv) In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

(v) Non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

DATA SECURITY
