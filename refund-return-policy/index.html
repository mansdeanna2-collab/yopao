RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we're here to help! Below is everything you need to know about our return and refund process.

RETURNS

Return Period: You have 30 days from the date of your purchase to request a return. Unfortunately, we cannot process returns after this period.

Eligibility: To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused and in the same condition as when you received it. It must also be in its original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Intimate or sanitary items
  • Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, or gases
  • Gift cards, downloadable software, and certain health/personal care items

Proof of Purchase: To complete a return, we'll need a receipt or proof of purchase.

Exchanges: We only replace items that are defective or damaged upon receipt. Please contact us for assistance if you need to exchange an item.

Partial Refunds

Only in certain cases, such as when:

  • The item shows signs of use or damage not caused by us
  • It's returned after 30 days
  • Items that are not in their original condition

REFUNDS

Once we receive and inspect your return, you'll be notified by email. If approved, your refund will be processed back to your original payment method. Refund processing times can vary depending on your bank or credit card company.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund:

  • Check your bank account and contact your bank or credit card company to see if there's any delay on their end.
  • If you've already done this and still haven't received your refund, please reach out to us, and we'll help track it.

SALE ITEMS

Only regular-priced items can be refunded. Unfortunately, sale items cannot be refunded.

GIFTS

If the item was a gift and marked as such at checkout, you will receive a gift credit for the return value. If the gift was shipped directly to you, we will send a gift certificate once the return is processed.

If the gift was purchased and shipped to the giver's address, the refund will be issued to them, and they will be notified of the return.

SHIPPING

Return Shipping: You are responsible for the return shipping costs. These costs are non-refundable, and if you receive a refund, the return shipping cost will be deducted from it.

Shipping Insurance: For returns over $75, we recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance as we cannot guarantee receipt of your returned item.

HOW TO RETURN YOUR ITEM

To initiate a return, please contact us using the form at the bottom of the website. You'll receive instructions on how to send your item back.

FAQ

How long do I have to request a return?
You have 30 days from the date of purchase.

How will my refund be processed?
Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout.

What condition must the item be in to return it?
The item must be in the same condition as when you received it, and in original packaging.

How do I initiate a return?
Please use the Contact Us section on the website.

Are there any charges for returns?
No, but you'll be responsible for the return shipping cost. Restocking fees do not apply.
